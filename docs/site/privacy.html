Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

Overview

ggcode is an open-source AI coding agent. We respect your privacy and minimize data collection. This policy applies to the ggcode CLI, desktop application, and mobile companion app (collectively, "ggcode").

Data We Collect

ggcode operates primarily on your local machine. We do not collect:

  • Source code or file contents processed by the agent
  • Chat messages between you and the AI
  • Terminal command outputs
  • Personal identification information

Data Stored Locally

ggcode stores the following on your device only:

  • Configuration files (~/.ggcode/ggcode.yaml) — API keys, provider settings
  • Session history (~/.ggcode/sessions/) — conversation logs in JSONL format
  • OAuth tokens (~/.ggcode/oauth-tokens/) — for provider authentication
  • Project memory (GGCODE.md, AGENTS.md) — project-specific notes

All local data remains under your full control. You can delete it at any time.

Third-Party Services

ggcode sends prompts to AI providers you configure (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google). These providers have their own privacy policies:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

We do not intermediate or log these communications. Your prompts go directly from your machine to the provider.

Mobile App

The ggcode mobile companion app connects directly to your desktop via WebSocket. No data passes through our servers. The mobile app:

  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not include ads or analytics trackers
  • Does not use in-app purchases
  • Communicates only with your own desktop computer

Desktop Relay

When using the relay feature for mobile connectivity, WebSocket connections may pass through a relay server. The relay forwards encrypted data without inspecting or storing message contents. No conversation data is persisted on relay servers.

Cookies

The ggcode.dev website does not use cookies or tracking scripts.

Open Source

ggcode is open source. You can audit the complete source code at github.com/topcheer/ggcode.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@ggcode.dev.
